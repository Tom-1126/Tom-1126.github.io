Liqiang Jing is a Ph.D. student at the University of Texas at Dallas. Liqiang Jing received the Master degree in the School of Computer Science and Technology from Shandong University in 2023 and the Bachelor degree in the School of Computer Science and Information from Hefei University of Technology in 2020. His research interests include multimodal learning and natural language processing. He has published several papers in the top venues (e.g., ACM SIGIR, ACM MM, AAAI, ACL, and IEEE TKDE). Moreover, he has served as a reviewer for several top conferences and journals, such as NeurIPS, TMM, ICML, TCSVT, ICLR, and Information Sciences.
