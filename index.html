Research on Error Correction-Oriented Knowledge Distillation

This is my Master's Thesis. In this research, we propose a knowledge distillation method based on error correction-oriented for image classification, dubbed as ECKD. It consists of three parts: a teacher network, a student network, and a bi-directional distillation module. The bi-directional distillation module works on realizing error correction-oriented knowledge distillation. Extensive experiments on CIFAR-100 dataset verify the effectiveness and advancement of our proposed method. At the same time, we further explore the knowledge distillation effect of different convolution stages of the model, which provides some reference value for future work.

Languages: Python(Pytorch)

2022.06

Early Warning of Hidden Danger of Transmission Line

This project provides a method for measuring the distance between hazards and the adjacent overhead transmission lines. It can identify the conductors and 11 common types of hazards. Furthermore, it calculates the actual distance between hazards and conductors based on on-site measurements of conductor heights. This project has been successfully implemented and applied in cities such as Zibo and Jinan in Shandong Province, China.

Languages: Python(Pytorch)
